Pizza

Description

This recipe is simple and uses common ingredients for a delicious homemade cheese pizza.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound pizza dough (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1 cup tomato sauce or ⅓ cup pizza sauce
  • 2 cups shredded Mozzarella cheese
  • ¼ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Roll or stretch the pizza dough on a floured surface to your desired size and thickness.
  2. Transfer the dough to a lightly greased baking sheet or pizza pan.
  3. Spread the sauce over the dough, leaving a border for the crust.
  4. Sprinkle the mozzarella and Parmesan cheeses over the sauce.
  5. Drizzle with olive oil and season with salt and pepper.
  6. Bake in a preheated oven at 450°F (230°C) or 475°F (245°C) for 12 to 15 minutes or 12 to 14 minutes, until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is bubbly.
  7. Let the pizza cool before slicing and serving.
