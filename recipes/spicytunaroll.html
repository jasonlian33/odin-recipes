Spicy Tuna Roll

Description

This recipe provides a simple approach to making a spicy tuna roll at home. It combines the flavors of fresh tuna with a creamy and spicy sauce, all wrapped in traditional sushi rice and seaweed.

Ingredients

  • Sashimi-grade tuna (finely chopped)
  • Mayonnaise (Japanese mayonnaise like Kewpie is recommended)
  • Siracha sauce
  • Green onions (chopped)
  • Sesame seeds (optional)
  • Soy Sauce (optional)
  • Nori sheets (dried seaweed)
  • Prepared sushi rice
  • Wasabi (optional)
  • Pickled ginger (optional)

Steps

  1. Prepare the Spicy Tuna Mix: In a bowl, combine the finely chopped sashimi-grade tuna, mayonnaise, sriracha sauce, and chopped green onions. You can adjust the amounts of mayonnaise and sriracha to control the creaminess and spiciness to your preference. Add sesame seeds and soy sauce to the mixture if desired.
  2. Prepare the Nori: Place a sheet of nori on a bamboo sushi mat (or a gallon size ziplock bag). You can use a whole sheet or cut it in half for smaller rolls. If using a sushi mat, consider wrapping it in plastic wrap for easier cleanup.
  3. Spread the Rice: Wet your hands with a mixture of water and a little rice vinegar to prevent the rice from sticking. Spread a thin, even layer of prepared sushi rice on the nori, leaving a space at the far edge. You can sprinkle sesame seeds over the rice if you like.
  4. Add the Tuna Mixture: Arrange the spicy tuna mixture in a line across the rice, about one-third of the way up from the bottom edge of the nori.
  5. Roll the Sushi: Lift the bottom edge of the bamboo mat and roll it forward, creating a tight cylinder. Apply firm pressure to make the roll compact.
  6. Slice the Roll: Use a sharp knife and wet it with water (or the vinegar water mixture) before slicing to make clean cuts. Cut the roll into bite-sized pieces.
  7. Serve: Arrange the sliced spicy tuna rolls on a plate and serve with soy sauce and wasabi. You can also include pickled ginger.
